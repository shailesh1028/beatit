About

Wildfire is India's oldest and the most prestigious rock band competition which turned international last year. With prizes worth lakhs of rupees and your prestige at stake, Wildfire has been the launch pad for many contemporary bands including -Underground Authority, Parikrama, Kryptos, Zygnema, Cassini's Division and many more. So be ready to fulfill your love for rock at the Wildfire Finals at Kharagpur! Wildfire is a western rock band competition, open to any college or semi-professional band from India and abroad.

Rules

1. Offline prelims will be conducted in Bangalore, Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, Guwahati and Kathmandu. Online prelims will also be conducted wherein participating bands can register.
2. After the first round, a shortlist of bands would be released who will qualify to perform at the main event at IIT Kharagpur.
3. The main event at IIT Kharagpur would consist of 2 rounds: prefinals and finals.
4. In prefinals at IIT Kharagpur, a group will be required to perform at least three numbers of their choice within 20 minutes. (Amps on to amps off and inclusive of sound check)
5. In finals, a group will be required to perform at least 4 numbers of their choice within 40 minutes (Amps on to amps off inclusive of sound check).
6. Bands will be disqualified on the spot for misconduct, obscenity or foul language, and will be banned from performing at all subsequent editions of spring fest.
7. Equipment provided will be:
• One bass amplifier speaker
• One lead amplifier speaker
• Adequate microphones
• Drum set (Participants can bring along with your own cymbals and chokes/high hats).
8. Bands will have to bring their own musical instruments and special effects. However, programmed music is not allowed.
9. Spring Fest will not be held responsible for the failure of any instrument.
The decision of the judges will be final and binding. The organizing committee reserves the right to make any last-minute change in the rules.
