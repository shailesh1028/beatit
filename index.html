About

Wildfire is India's oldest and the most prestigious rock band competition which turned international last year. With prizes worth lakhs of rupees and your prestige at stake, Wildfire has been the launch pad for many contemporary bands including -Underground Authority, Parikrama, Kryptos, Zygnema, Cassini's Division and many more.
So be ready to fulfill your love for rock at the Wildfire Finals at Kharagpur!, Wildfire is a western rock band competition, open to any college or semi-professional band from India and abroad.

Rules

1. The competition will be in two parts- Prelims and Finals.
2. Prelims will be held online wherein top 16 will be selected and called up for finals in Kharagpur during the fest.
3. In case a participant is not able to attend, the next participant will be approached or finally on spot registrations will be done in case the minimum number of participants is not reached.
4. Decision of the judges will be final and binding. No changes will be made once the judges have announced the results.
5. No foul language is allowed. Participant may receive a penalty or not allowed to compete whichever is deemed fit by the organizing committe.

Prelims

1. The participants will be required to send a YouTube video of not more than 1.5 minutes performing their piece and submit it in the portal to be released on the FB Page.
2. No musical instruments will be allowed. In case it is found, the participant will be disqualified.

Finals

1. This will be a 1vs1 battle rounds among the top 16 participants wherein each beatboxer would be given 2 minutes each and the winners will be decided by the judges.
2. The winner from each battle will be promoted to the next battle and the opponents will be decided by the judges whichever way they seem fit.
3. No musical instruments will be allowed. In case it is found, the participant will be disqualified.
4. In case judges are not able to decide, another 30 seconds will be provided to each participant to perform. It will be avoided whenever possible.
